Slow Cooked Pot Roast.

Slow cooked pot roast recipe and cooking instructions.

Pot roast has been a staple for busy families as an easy, low maintenance meal. Pot roast is highly
nutritious hearty option for any busy family. And the best part about it is you can let it sit and
let it cook all day until it is ready to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of golden mini potatoes.
  • 2 stalks of chopped celery
  • 2 large carrots peeled and cut.
  • 1 can of V8 vegetable juice.
  • 1 chuck roast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion powder.
  • Rosemary
  • Half of a yellow onion. Chopped up.
  • Parsley

Cooking instructions.

  1. To start add wet ingredients first. Pour one 12 oz can of V8 juice into the crock-pot.
  2. Next chop up 2 stocks of celery and add to crock pot.
  3. Peel and chop 2 large carrots into preferable bite sized pieces.
  4. Add 1tsp of salt and 2tsps of pepper.
  5. Add chuck roast.
  6. Add 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  7. Add 1 tsp of onion powder.
  8. Add 1 tbsp of rosemary.
  9. Add 1 tbsp of parsley.
  10. Add half of a chopped yellow onion. I like to mince mine so it breaks down while it is cooking.
