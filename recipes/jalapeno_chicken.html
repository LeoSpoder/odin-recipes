Jalapeno Popper Stuffed bacon wrapped Chicken

Ingredients

  • Large chicken Breast
  • 1 pack of bacon, I use oscar meyer center cut.
  • One pack of whipped cream cheese, I use whipped because it is easier to mix.
  • One pack of mexican blend shredded cheese, or ghost pepper shredded cheese if you like it hot!
  • One Jalapeno for every chicken breast you plan on cooking, make sure they are plump.
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. This recipe is for 6 chicken breasts, adjust accordingly
  2. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese and 1 cup of shredded cheese. Mix in bowl
  3. Filet jalapenos to remove stem and seeds and clear space for filling
  4. Add cream cheese filling to jalapenos and press them back together
  5. Prepare 6 Chicken breast by fileting them and leaving them open.
  6. Put Poppers inside of fileted chicken breasts and close them up.
  7. Next wrap the entire chicken breast in bacon, being careful not to over lap so the bacon cooks evenly
  8. Cook at 400 to an internal temperature of 165 degrees F
  9. Let rest for five minutes before enjoying!
