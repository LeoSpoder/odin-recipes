Marry me Tortellini

/>

Marry me tortellini is an exciting way to spice up a plain old chicken breast. With cheese filled noodles and
out of this world sauce, this is going to become a new household favorite. There is some prep work that goes
into it but nothing the average household cook can't crank out in 30 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Flour
  • Butter and oil
  • Shallot
  • Garlic
  • Sun dried tomatoes in olive oil (regular sun dried tomatoes will work as well
  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken broth
  • Cheese tortellini
  • Heavy cream
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan cheese

Recipe

  1. Season and dredge the chicken. Cut chicken into pretty small chunks
    and place in a medium bowl. Season with salt, pepper, italian seasoning
    and smoked paprika. Toss to get the chicken coated in the seasonings add the
    flower and gently toss to lightly coat chicken
  2. Heat large skillet over medium high heat
  3. Add the butter and one tablespoon of olive oil.
  4. When oil is up to temp add chicken in an even layer. Cook the chicken
    tossing two or three times. Remove it to a bowl or plate
  5. Add 1 table spoon of olive oil and the garlic, shallot, and sun dried
    tomatoes. Saute for three minutes, stirring often, until softened and fragrant.
  6. Season with red pepper flakes and another pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. Add the tomato paste and broth. stir to combine, use spatula to scrape
    all those yummy bits on the bottom of the skillet.
  8. Stir in the uncooked tortellini, bring to a simmer and reduce heat to
    to maintain a slow simmer. Cover and let simmer for 2-3 minutes until tortellini
    is cooked. Be sure to stir at the 2 minute mark to ensure even cooking
  9. Remove the lid and stir in the chicken, spinach, cream and parmesan cheese until
    everything is well combined. Serve hot with fresh basil, more parmesan, freshly
    cracked black pepper.
  10. Last step is to enjoy!
